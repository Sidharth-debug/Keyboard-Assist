Piano Learning Tool

Helping beginners learn the piano with LED light cues!

Introduction

The Piano Learning Tool is designed to assist keyboard and piano beginners in mastering their instrument. By using visual cues (simulated LED lights), learners are guided to play the correct keys at the right time. Whether you are a complete beginner or just need some extra help, this tool is perfect for accelerating your learning process.

How It Works

Our innovative tool uses a piano keyboard interface with visual LED cues that light up the correct keys to play. The learner follows these cues by clicking on the highlighted keys in sequence, helping to improve hand-eye coordination and memory. The tool also allows you to manually progress to the next note, offering a flexible learning experience.

Features

Interactive Piano Keys

Click on the piano keys to practice the right notes. The keys light up to indicate the correct note to play.

Visual LED Cues

Simulated LED lights highlight the correct keys to play, helping beginners to follow along easily.

Progress Tracking

Keep track of your progress by advancing through lessons as you master each note.
