About the Inventors

Meet the passionate creators behind the Piano Learning Tool

Our Story

We are a group of engineers and musicians who wanted to create an accessible tool to help beginners learn how to play the piano. After working together for months, we combined our skills in technology and music to develop the Piano Learning Tool. Our aim is to make learning the piano easier, more engaging, and fun!
